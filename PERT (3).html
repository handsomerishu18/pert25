Prioritize tasks:

The most critical tasks are Task 1, Task 2, Task 3, and Task 4, which provide the foundational knowledge required for web development.

Estimate time and resources:

Estimate the time required for each task based on the difficulty and complexity of the task. Allocate appropriate resources, such as books, online courses, and practice exercises.

Identify milestones:

Milestones might include completing each subtask, completing a module, or completing a project.

Review and update regularly:

Review and update the PERT chart regularly to reflect your progress and adjust your plan as needed.
